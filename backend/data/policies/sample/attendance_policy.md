# Attendance Policy

## 1. Purpose

This policy establishes the attendance requirements for all employees to ensure smooth business operations and maintain productivity standards.

## 2. Working Hours

### 2.1 Standard Working Hours
- **Core Hours**: 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM (mandatory presence)
- **Flexible Hours**: 9:00 AM - 10:00 AM and 4:00 PM - 6:00 PM
- **Total Hours**: Minimum 8 hours per day, 40 hours per week
- **Break Time**: 1 hour lunch break (12:30 PM - 1:30 PM recommended)

### 2.2 Shift Timings (for applicable roles)

| Shift | Start Time | End Time |
|-------|-----------|----------|
| General | 9:00 AM | 6:00 PM |
| Morning | 7:00 AM | 4:00 PM |
| Evening | 2:00 PM | 11:00 PM |
| Night | 10:00 PM | 7:00 AM |

## 3. Attendance Recording

### 3.1 Check-in/Check-out
- All employees must record attendance through the SAMVIT HRMS biometric/digital system
- Check-in must be done within 15 minutes of shift start time
- Check-out must be done at shift end

### 3.2 Remote Work Attendance
- Remote employees must log in through the SAMVIT mobile app or web portal
- First login of the day is considered check-in time
- Last activity log is considered check-out time

## 4. Punctuality

### 4.1 Grace Period
- 15-minute grace period is allowed for check-in
- More than 3 late arrivals (beyond grace period) in a month will be counted as half-day absence

### 4.2 Late Arrival Consequences
| Late Instances (per month) | Action |
|---------------------------|--------|
| 1-3 times | Warning |
| 4-5 times | 0.5 day salary deduction |
| 6+ times | 1 day salary deduction + formal warning |

## 5. Absence Without Information

- Failure to inform about absence before shift start is considered unauthorized absence
- Three consecutive days of unauthorized absence may lead to disciplinary action
- Repeated unauthorized absences can result in termination

## 6. Work From Home (WFH)

### 6.1 Eligibility
- Employees who have completed 3 months of service
- Role must be eligible for remote work
- Manager approval required

### 6.2 WFH Guidelines
- Maximum 2 days WFH per week (unless otherwise specified)
- Must be reachable during core hours
- Video calls should have camera on
- Must have stable internet connection

## 7. Overtime

### 7.1 Overtime Eligibility
- Overtime applies to non-managerial employees
- Must be pre-approved by manager
- Compensatory off or overtime pay as per company policy

### 7.2 Overtime Rates
- Weekdays: 1.5x hourly rate
- Weekends: 2x hourly rate
- Public Holidays: 2.5x hourly rate

## 8. Attendance Reports

- Monthly attendance reports are generated on the 1st of each month
- Employees can view their attendance in the SAMVIT self-service portal
- Discrepancies must be reported within 5 working days

## 9. Exceptions

- Medical emergencies with documentation
- Natural calamities or civil disturbances
- Public transport strikes (with evidence)
- Any other force majeure events

## 10. Compliance

Non-compliance with this policy may result in:
1. Verbal warning
2. Written warning
3. Salary deductions
4. Performance review impact
5. Termination (in severe cases)
