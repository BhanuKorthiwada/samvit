# Employee Benefits Policy

## 1. Overview

This document outlines the benefits provided to all eligible employees of the organization. Benefits are an integral part of the total compensation package.

## 2. Eligibility

- Regular full-time employees are eligible from date of joining
- Contract employees may have modified benefits (as per contract)
- Part-time employees receive prorated benefits
- Benefits continue through the notice period

## 3. Health Insurance

### 3.1 Medical Insurance
- **Coverage**: Employee + Spouse + 2 Children + Parents
- **Sum Insured**: ₹5,00,000 per family per year
- **Premium**: Fully paid by company
- **Network Hospitals**: 5000+ across India

### 3.2 Coverage Includes
- Hospitalization expenses
- Pre and post hospitalization (60 days)
- Day care procedures
- Maternity benefits (after 9 months of waiting period)
- New-born coverage
- Ambulance charges up to ₹2,000

### 3.3 Exclusions
- Pre-existing diseases (first 4 years)
- Cosmetic surgery
- Dental treatment (unless accident-related)
- Self-inflicted injuries

## 4. Life Insurance

### 4.1 Group Term Life Insurance
- **Coverage**: 3x Annual CTC
- **Nominee**: As per employee designation
- **Premium**: Fully paid by company

### 4.2 Accidental Death & Disability
- **Coverage**: 2x Annual CTC
- Covers permanent total and partial disability

## 5. Retirement Benefits

### 5.1 Provident Fund (PF)
- Employee contribution: 12% of Basic + DA
- Employer contribution: 12% of Basic + DA
- Interest rate: As per EPFO guidelines

### 5.2 Gratuity
- Eligible after 5 years of continuous service
- Calculation: (15/26) × Last Drawn Basic × Years of Service
- Maximum limit as per Gratuity Act

### 5.3 National Pension Scheme (NPS) - Optional
- Employee can contribute additional 10% of Basic
- Company matches up to 10% of Basic
- Tax benefits under Section 80CCD

## 6. Leaves and Time Off

Refer to the Leave Policy document for detailed information on:
- Casual Leave (12 days)
- Sick Leave (12 days)
- Earned Leave (15 days)
- Maternity/Paternity Leave
- Bereavement Leave

## 7. Learning and Development

### 7.1 Training Budget
- ₹50,000 per employee per year
- Can be used for certifications, courses, conferences
- Requires manager approval

### 7.2 Education Assistance
- Up to ₹1,00,000 for job-related degrees
- Reimbursed upon successful completion
- Service bond of 2 years applies

## 8. Wellness Benefits

### 8.1 Gym/Fitness Allowance
- ₹1,500 per month reimbursement
- Valid for gym memberships, yoga classes, sports activities

### 8.2 Mental Health Support
- Free access to counseling services
- 6 sessions per year
- Confidential Employee Assistance Program (EAP)

### 8.3 Annual Health Checkup
- Comprehensive health checkup once a year
- Fully covered by company
- Choice of empaneled hospitals

## 9. Work-Life Benefits

### 9.1 Flexible Working
- Work from Home options available
- Flexible timing within core hours
- Part-time options for new parents

### 9.2 Parental Benefits
- Creche reimbursement up to ₹5,000/month
- Nursing room facility available
- Parental support groups

## 10. Financial Benefits

### 10.1 Employee Referral Bonus
- ₹25,000 - ₹75,000 based on position level
- Paid after referee completes 3 months

### 10.2 Performance Bonus
- Up to 20% of annual CTC
- Based on individual and company performance
- Paid annually

### 10.3 Internet Allowance (Remote Workers)
- ₹1,000 per month
- For approved remote work arrangements

## 11. Other Benefits

- Sodexo meal vouchers: ₹2,200/month
- Mobile phone allowance: ₹1,000/month (eligible roles)
- Transportation allowance: ₹1,600/month
- Subsidized cafeteria
- Free parking
- Employee discounts with partner brands

## 12. Changes to Benefits

The company reserves the right to modify benefits with reasonable notice. Major changes will be communicated at least 30 days in advance.
