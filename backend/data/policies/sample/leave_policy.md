# Leave Policy

## 1. Overview

This policy outlines the leave entitlements, eligibility criteria, and procedures for requesting leave at our organization. All employees are expected to familiarize themselves with this policy and adhere to the guidelines specified.

## 2. Types of Leave

### 2.1 Casual Leave (CL)
- **Entitlement**: 12 days per year
- **Accumulation**: Casual leaves cannot be carried forward to the next year
- **Minimum Notice**: 1 day prior (except emergencies)
- **Maximum Consecutive Days**: 3 days

### 2.2 Sick Leave (SL)
- **Entitlement**: 12 days per year
- **Accumulation**: Up to 30 days can be carried forward
- **Medical Certificate**: Required for sick leave exceeding 2 consecutive days
- **Immediate family illness**: Can be used for immediate family member's illness (with documentation)

### 2.3 Earned Leave / Privilege Leave (EL/PL)
- **Entitlement**: 15 days per year (accrued at 1.25 days per month)
- **Accumulation**: Can be carried forward up to 45 days maximum
- **Notice Period**: Minimum 7 days advance notice required
- **Minimum Duration**: 3 consecutive days

### 2.4 Maternity Leave
- **Entitlement**: 26 weeks (for first two children)
- **Eligibility**: Female employees who have worked for at least 80 days in the 12 months before the expected delivery date
- **Additional Leave**: 12 weeks for third child or adoption

### 2.5 Paternity Leave
- **Entitlement**: 5 days
- **Eligibility**: Male employees for the birth of their child
- **Must be taken**: Within 3 months of child's birth

### 2.6 Bereavement Leave
- **Entitlement**: 3 days for immediate family, 1 day for extended family
- **Immediate Family**: Spouse, children, parents, siblings
- **Extended Family**: Grandparents, in-laws, aunts, uncles

## 3. Leave Application Procedure

1. Submit leave request through the SAMVIT HRMS system
2. Ensure adequate handover of responsibilities
3. Get approval from reporting manager before leave commencement
4. For unplanned leaves, inform manager immediately and regularize within 24 hours of returning

## 4. Leave Approval Hierarchy

| Leave Duration | Approval Authority |
|---------------|-------------------|
| 1-3 days | Reporting Manager |
| 4-7 days | Department Head |
| 8+ days | HR + Department Head |

## 5. Leave Restrictions

- Leave cannot be combined with official holidays without prior approval
- Leave during probation period requires special approval
- Leave during notice period may not be approved (except medical emergencies)
- Public holidays falling within leave period will not be counted as leave days
